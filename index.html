About Me

Updated on Dec 16, 2017

I have been a Research Assistant and Doctoral Researcher in Manchester Institute of Innovation Research (MIOIR) at Alliance Manchester Business School, the University of Manchester since September 2013. My research interest mainly lies in innovation policies, innovation systems, and innovation strategies in Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs), especially in emerging technologies and emerging markets.

I am also the President of China Students and Scholars Assocation in Manchester (CSSA-Manchester), under the Student Union of University of Manchester, and the Vice President of China Students and Scholars Assocation in UK (CSSA-UK) .
